Case Study 01 · Procore Technologies

Enterprise Agentic AI Platform — 0% to 90%+ Response Accuracy

GenAI / AgenticProduction3,000+ Customers
+

Problem

Procore's AI assistant was returning ~45% accurate responses — hallucinating project data, surfacing wrong documents, and failing on multi-domain queries. Enterprise customers were churning on AI features before they could demonstrate ROI. The platform had no grounding strategy and no evaluation framework to measure or improve.

Strategy & Execution

  • Designed supervisor orchestration layer with intent-routing to domain-specific agents via real-time manifest resolution.
  • Built just-in-time grounding workflows with schema-driven action layers to eliminate hallucinations.
  • Established Evaluation-as-a-Service with transaction-level metrics: correctness, recall, and completeness.
  • Shipped multimodal RAG pipelines supporting text, document, and image intelligence across all enterprise tiers.
  • Defined AI governance framework: agent registries, versioning, ML exclusions, and hybrid-cloud compliance across AWS, GCP, Azure.

License-to-Subscription Migration — $1B ARR Platform

Platform / API$1B ARRBusiness Transformation
+

Problem

Autodesk was pivoting from perpetual licenses to a subscription model — one of the most complex commercial transformations a software company can undertake. The existing infrastructure had no transactional API layer to support subscription creation, entitlement management, or partner order placement at scale.

Strategy & Execution

  • Led design of transactional subscription APIs — GET/POST microservices for partner order placement, pricing, and product management.

  • Built order-to-subscription sync pipeline: .com → SAP (order creation) → Salesforce (subscription with entitlements and product line items).
  • Delivered AI/ML churn and buying-readiness models contributing to 25% YoY revenue growth.
  • Architected NLP-powered support bot (IBM Watson) resolving ~20K cases/month, saving $1M+ annually.

Case Study 03 · Procore Technologies

360° Executive Dashboard — Near-Real-Time Reporting at Scale

Data PlatformDistributed SystemsCAP Theorem Applied
+

Problem

Enterprise customers ($500M+ ARR accounts) were threatening non-renewal because Procore had no cross-domain reporting. Data was fragmented across Financials, PMQS, and Resource Management. Full materialization took 45 minutes — customers needed data in under 5 minutes.

Strategy & Execution

  • Designed partition strategy: top 50 enterprise customers got dedicated high-volume partitions; remaining customers shared an optimized partition.
  • Introduced incremental materialization across all domains — trading bounded staleness for 5-minute data availability.
  • Audited and migrated DynamoDB → DocumentDB → native MongoDB to achieve <250ms P99 SLO on nested aggregations.
  • Committed to full consistency within 10 minutes via incremental batch processing — a tradeoff customers explicitly approved.
  • Built cross-domain reporting models joining Financials, PMQS, and Resource Management into a unified 360° view.
